Running a cafe is hectic. Orders coming in, receipts to print, inventory to track, money to count. Manganin takes the chaos out of cafe management—a modern dashboard that handles orders, tracks what's selling, prints receipts, and gives you the financial reports you actually need. Everything in one place, nothing slowing you down.

## What It Does

- **Smart Order Management** – Handle dine-in and take-away orders smoothly without the confusion
- **Receipt Printing** – Generate and print receipts instantly, no formatting headaches
- **Sales Intelligence** – See exactly which items fly off the shelf and which ones don't
- **Financial Clarity** – Complete financial reports so you know exactly where your money is going
- **Transaction Tracking** – Every transaction logged and organized, ready to audit
- **Beautiful Interface** – Modern design that's actually pleasant to use all day

## Key Features

- **Real-time Order Processing** – Dine-in and take-away orders with instant updates
- **Receipt Generation** – Print-ready receipts formatted and ready to go
- **Sales Analytics** – Track best-selling items and menu performance
- **Financial Reports** – Detailed breakdowns of revenue, expenses, and profit
- **Transaction History** – Complete audit trail of all transactions
- **Intuitive Dashboard** – Clean, modern interface built for restaurant staff
- **Settings Management** – Customize your cafe's setup easily

## Built With

- **Next.js** – React framework that keeps everything fast and responsive
- **Material UI** – Beautiful, professional UI components out of the box
- **React** – Component-based architecture for a smooth experience
- **TypeScript** – Catches errors early, keeps the code maintainable

## Why It Matters

Cafe owners deserve better tools. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, PDFs, and handwritten notes, Manganin brings everything together. You get real insights into your business, faster order processing, and time to actually focus on making great coffee instead of managing paperwork.
